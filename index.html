about me.

hello, my name is Carl Czarnetzki. I'm a 20-year-old student at the OTH Regensburg, where I study General Computer Science in the second semester. I'm from Hof an der Saale, but I moved to Regensburg recently. On this website I want to share my interests, hobbies and other cool content I like.
Have fun :)

hobbies.

electric guitar.

when I was little, my dad gave me a cd with "Band Of Gypsys" by Jimi Hendrix on it. Since then, I was hooked on electric guitars. First I learned to play classical guitar, but that got boring and after a while I started playing electric guitar. Currently, I got my little set-up with a few paddles, an amp and my beloved Fender Stratocaster. Most of the time I just freestyle and have fun, but I'm not nearly where I want to be. The funnest part is jamming with friends, although we are just experimenting most of the time.
